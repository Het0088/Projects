Frequently Asked Questions

What are your adoption hours?

Our adoption center is open Monday through Friday from 9AM to 6PM, and Saturday from 10AM to 4PM. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays.

Do I need an appointment to meet a pet?

While walk-ins are welcome, we encourage scheduling an appointment to ensure staff availability and to give you the best experience possible.

What should I bring when coming to adopt?

Please bring a valid ID, proof of residence (such as a utility bill), and if you're a renter, a copy of your lease showing pet allowance.

How can I volunteer with Streeties?

We welcome volunteers! Please fill out our volunteer form on the website or contact us directly to learn about current opportunities.
