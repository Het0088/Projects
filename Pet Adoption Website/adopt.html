Frequently Asked Questions

What are the adoption fees?

Our adoption fees range from ₹1000-2000, which includes vaccinations, deworming, and spaying/neutering.

What's included in the adoption?

All pets come vaccinated, dewormed, and spayed/neutered. They also receive a health check before adoption.

How long does the process take?

The adoption process typically takes 3-7 days, including home checks and paperwork.

Do you offer post-adoption support?

Yes! We provide guidance and support even after adoption to ensure a smooth transition.
