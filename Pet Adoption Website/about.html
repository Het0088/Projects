Our Mission

Giving Every Street Animal a Loving Home

Who We Are

Streeties is a passionate community of animal lovers dedicated to transforming the lives of street animals in India. Since 2020, we've been working tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome our local furry friends.

Our team consists of dedicated volunteers, veterinarians, and animal welfare experts who believe every animal deserves a chance at happiness.

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every animal with love and care, ensuring their well-being is our top priority.

Community

We work together with local communities to create a better world for street animals.

Commitment

We are dedicated to providing the best possible care and finding perfect forever homes.
